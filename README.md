# DEST (Proposal Design)

A simple design proposal for DEST — a free electric ride-share landing page concept for San Antonio, powered by local business advertising
- Bold hero with service tagline and dual CTAs ("Book a Party Ride" / "How It Works")
- Interactive party rental booking calendar with reservation form and success toast
- FAQ, about section, and full responsive layout with eco-friendly branding
- Advertiser section showcasing vehicle wraps and iPad screen ad tiers targeting local businesses
- No pricing
